MUD\WTR is a functional beverage company that offers a healthier alternative to coffee. Their flagship product is a cacao-based drink infused with a blend of organic mushrooms, including chaga, reishi, lion's mane, and cordyceps, providing a natural energy boost and a variety of health benefits. MUD\WTR is committed to sustainability, using American-grown mushrooms and globally sourced ingredients, and offers a variety of flavors, including original, matcha, turmeric, and rest.  MUD\WTR offers a subscription service to ensure customers never run out of their favorite blend, and they also have a cafe called MUD\WTR Gather where customers can enjoy their products in a social setting.  MUD\WTR is passionate about supporting the safe and careful use of psychedelics and has donated to psychedelic research.
